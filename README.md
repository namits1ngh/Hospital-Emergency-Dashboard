Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard | Power BI:

Developed an interactive Hospital Emergency Room Analytics Dashboard in Microsoft Power BI to analyze 9,216 patient records from April 2023 to October 2024. The dashboard provides monthly and consolidated views of key hospital performance indicators, including patient volume, average wait time, satisfaction scores, referrals, admission status, and patient demographics.

Built interactive date, year, and month filters with dedicated pages for Monthly Analysis, Consolidated Analysis, Patient Details, and Key Takeaways. Created KPI cards, trend charts, demographic breakdowns, referral analysis, and a day-and-hour heatmap to identify peak emergency room periods.

Key insights identified:
Analyzed 9,216 patients with an average wait time of 35.3 minutes and an average satisfaction score of 4.99/10.
Found that approximately 69% of patients were seen within 30 minutes.
Identified Monday as the busiest day, with 1,377 patients.
Analyzed admission patterns, with 4,612 patients admitted and 4,604 not admitted.
Found General Practice and Orthopedics to be the most common referral departments.
Examined patient distribution across age groups, gender, race, admission status, department referrals, days, and hours to support resource allocation decisions.
